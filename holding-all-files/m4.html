BMW M4 CSL

The BMW M4 CSL offers a thrilling driving experience with its track-focused performance and lightweight design. Boasting 543 horsepower, precision handling, and aggressive styling, it's ideal for drivers who crave a high-performance sports car for the road or track.

Stats

  • Horsepower: 543 hp
  • Top Spead: 191 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 3.6 seconds
  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Inline-6
  • Seating: 2 passengers (due to weight reduction in the CSL model, the rear seats are removed)
